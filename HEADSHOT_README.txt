# Headshot Image

Replace this file with your professional headshot photo.

## Requirements:
- Filename: `headshot.jpg`
- Recommended size: 640x640 pixels or larger
- Format: JPG or PNG (if PNG, rename to headshot.jpg)
- Aspect ratio: Square (1:1)
- Professional quality photo with good lighting

## Tips for a great headshot:
- Face the camera directly
- Use professional attire
- Choose a clean, neutral background
- Ensure good, even lighting
- Smile naturally
- High resolution (at least 1200x1200px for best quality)

Once you have your photo:
1. Name it `headshot.jpg`
2. Replace this file with your photo
3. The website will automatically display it in the hero section
