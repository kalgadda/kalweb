# Resume PDF

Add your resume PDF to this location.

## Requirements:
- Filename: `resume.pdf`
- Format: PDF
- Recommended: Under 5MB for fast downloads

## How to add your resume:
1. Export your resume as a PDF from Word, Google Docs, or your resume builder
2. Name it exactly: `resume.pdf`
3. Place it in this folder (same location as index.html)
4. The website will automatically enable the download functionality

## Resume tips:
- Keep it to 1-2 pages
- Use a clean, professional format
- Ensure all text is selectable (not scanned images)
- Include your contact information
- Highlight your key achievements and metrics
- Proofread carefully

The resume is accessible via:
- "Download Resume" button in navigation
- "Download Resume" button in the contact section
